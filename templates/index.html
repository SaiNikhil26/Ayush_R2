Some common questions
were often asked

How does the plant identification feature work?

We utilize Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) technology to identify plants We reference data from the Indian botanical website.

What types of images work best for plant identification?

Clear and well-lit photos of plants tend to yield the most accurate identification results.

How accurate is the plant identification?

Our model achieves an accuracy rate of 95% in plant identification. Please note that accuracy can vary based on image quality and plant complexity, so expert consultation or dedicated plant identification apps are advisable for critical tasks.

How long does it take to identify a plant?

The identification process typically takes a few minutes(~2min) to provide results.

Are my data and uploaded images safe and secure?

We prioritize data security. Your information and images are safely stored in our database.

What sets your plant identification service apart from others?

Our service is distinguished by its use of CNN technology and the extensive Indian botanical database as a reference, offering a unique and reliable plant identification experience.
